Simon Rogers

Simon is an AI data scientist at NHS National Services Scotland, and an honorary lecturer in Computer Science at the University of Glasgow. His research focuses on machine-learning and statistical techniques for complex data, particularly in metabolomics, HCI, and information retrieval.

Justin van der Hooft

After a PhD in Wageningen, Justin moved to Glasgow Polyomics and later became Assistant Professor in Computational Metabolomics at Wageningen University. He co-founded the MS2LDA project and continues to develop natural-language techniques for metabolome mining. Publications are listed here.

Joe Wandy

Joe is a data scientist at Glasgow Polyomics. His PhD in Computing Science explored natural-language processing and large-scale ML for biological data.
