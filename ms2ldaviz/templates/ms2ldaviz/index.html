12 June 2025: We are delighted to unveil a brand-new, Dash-powered interactive interface for exploring Mass2Motifs. Access it at https://ms2lda.org/viz/. The dashboard offers faster navigation, richer plots and a modern code base. We intend to retire the legacy MS2LDAviz application soon, so please update your workflows and bookmarks. Thank you to the community for your continued enthusiasm and to Glasgow Polyomics for their generous support.

This should hopefully allow for a more robust service in the future. Unfortunately during the migration process, all jobs submitted on the system from 25 September to now (11th October) were not working. If you tried to submit a job during that time period, your job will silently fail and/or it will show as ‚ÄúPending‚Äù status forever.
